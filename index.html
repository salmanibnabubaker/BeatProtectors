BeatProtectors

Instructions

  • grid contains two types of blocks
    • not blocked-through which user can move
    • blocked-act as walls
  • 3 protectors will be released from cave.
  • enemy vision is restricted to 1 layer around their position
  • they will wander through grid endlessly.
  • they can catch you.results in lose of life
  • you've got 3 chances in total
  • you can defend enemy vision by pressing key-'D' at layer-1 around their position
  • you can attack enemy at their postion using key-'A'.killing them results in locking them in cave for 5s
  • losing life will result in 3s delay for replaying again
  • aim to collect many treasures as you can-points 50
  • killing enemies-points 25

start game
